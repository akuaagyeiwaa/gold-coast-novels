The Novels

Marita or the Folly of Love (1885–1888)

Explores themes of love, tradition, and colonial influence in 19th-century Ghana.

Ethiopia Unbound (1911)

A blend of fiction and essay promoting African identity and resistance to imperialism.

The Anglo-Fanti (1928)

A witty satire of colonial mimicry among Ghana’s educated elite.

Eighteenpence (1943)

Critiques colonial justice systems and explores tensions between kinship and power.
